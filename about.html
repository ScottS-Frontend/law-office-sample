A Legacy of Legal Excellence in Solano County

Founded by attorney Robert Sample, our firm began with a simple mission: provide accessible, high-quality legal representation to families and businesses in our community.

What started as a solo practice has grown into a respected team of attorneys, each bringing specialized expertise and a shared commitment to client advocacy. We've helped hundreds of clients navigate complex legal challenges, from contentious divorces to high-stakes criminal trials.

Our approach combines aggressive courtroom litigation with strategic negotiation, ensuring every client receives tailored representation designed to achieve the best possible outcome.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Transparent communication and honest counsel in every interaction.
  • Tenacity: Relentless pursuit of justice, whether in settlement or trial.
  • Compassion: Understanding the human impact behind every legal matter.
  • Excellence: Continuous learning and meticulous preparation.
