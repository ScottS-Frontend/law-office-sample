What We Offer

Five Areas of Expertise

Every case deserves specialized attention. Our attorneys bring deep experience across five core practice areas, ensuring you receive knowledgeable representation tailored to your specific legal needs.

Practice Area 01

Family Law

Compassionate guidance through life's most challenging transitions.

Family legal matters require both legal precision and emotional sensitivity. We help clients navigate divorce, custody disputes, adoption, and support modifications with a focus on protecting what matters most: your family.

  • Divorce & Legal Separation
  • Child Custody & Visitation
  • Child & Spousal Support
  • Adoption & Guardianship
  • Prenuptial & Postnuptial Agreements
  • Domestic Violence Restraining Orders

Practice Area 02

Criminal Defense

Aggressive defense to protect your freedom and future.

A criminal charge can change everything. Our defense team has secured hundreds of dismissals, acquittals, and reduced charges. We investigate every detail, challenge evidence, and fight relentlessly for your rights.

  • DUI & DWI Defense
  • Drug Crimes (Possession, Distribution, Trafficking)
  • Theft, Burglary & Robbery
  • Assault & Battery
  • White-Collar Crimes (Fraud, Embezzlement)
  • Expungement & Record Sealing

Practice Area 03

Personal Injury

Maximum compensation for your injuries and losses.

When someone else's negligence causes you harm, you deserve full compensation—not lowball insurance offers. We handle every aspect of your claim, from investigation to trial, so you can focus on healing.

  • Car, Truck & Motorcycle Accidents
  • Slip & Fall / Premises Liability
  • Medical Malpractice
  • Wrongful Death
  • Workplace Injuries
  • Dog Bites & Animal Attacks

Practice Area 04

Estate Planning

Secure your legacy and protect your loved ones.

Proper estate planning ensures your wishes are honored and your family is protected. We create comprehensive plans that minimize taxes, avoid probate complications, and provide peace of mind for generations.

  • Wills & Living Trusts
  • Durable Power of Attorney
  • Advance Healthcare Directives
  • Probate Administration
  • Asset Protection Strategies
  • Special Needs Trusts

Practice Area 05

Business Law

Strategic counsel to help your business thrive.

From startup formation to complex litigation, we provide the legal foundation businesses need to grow. Our proactive approach helps you avoid disputes while positioning your company for long-term success.

  • Business Formation (LLC, Corp, Partnership)
  • Contracts & Negotiations
  • Employment Agreements
  • Commercial Disputes & Litigation
  • Intellectual Property Protection
  • Mergers & Acquisitions
